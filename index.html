Biografy (or kinda)

I'm a Braziliam guy that aways was in love by games, computer and everything that was tech. When I was a child, my mom usually worked from 6:00 AM till dusk. Then, to not be alone after the classroom, I went to computer.

I dont know exactly when I discovered my passion for programming. With 13 years, I remember that I started making simple websites, just with HTML for fun. After that, went to Pascal and made a basic calculator.

None of them was quite impressive, but I really had fun working with these projects

Education

My city was in a region of Brasil called by "Vale do Jequitionha". It is known by your poverty.
My elementary school was, initially, in a public school. But the quality was not that good, then my mom prefered to enroll myself in a private school

There, she meet the school staff and create a strong bond with the supervisor

After 2 years, the quality of the direction was very bad, and my Mom, with the supervisor that she meet created a new school, where I studied until my High school degree.

University

In the High school, I felt in love with mathematic and physics. The computer engineering fitted like a glove in my interests

I apllied for the University of São Paulo (USP), one of the greatest Universitys in Latin America and happily, was accepted!

The COVID comes to difficult everything, and initially, I wasn't able to go presencially in the classes.

Luckily, the university has a really good infraestruct and I could watch everything online from my city

In 2022, the presencially activities returned and here I am, writing this site from the library of USP!
